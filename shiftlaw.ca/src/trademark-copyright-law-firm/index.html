A tailored approach that works for you

We customize our trademark and copyright strategies to align with each client’s needs rather than fitting each client into a standard approach. Our clients are typically entrepreneurial and they come in all sizes, each with its own needs, from global corporations to owner-operated Canadian small businesses.

The attention you deserve

Clients who may be too small to get attention at big firms get to feel like a big deal at our boutique law firm. No one should feel like they are competing for attention with bigger clients. At Shift Law, every client gets priority treatment and personalized service.

A return on your investment

We only provide services where we can provide a return on our client’s legal spend. We will never recommend work that is likely to cost more than it’s worth and we ensure that our clients are always aware of potential costs before they are incurred.

Streamlined client service

We use a distinctive streamlined approach to client service, from intake to invoicing and everything in between. Our focus on what matters and what doesn’t enables us to work quickly without sacrificing quality.

More than just legal services – a trusted partner

Each of our IP professionals is passionate about what we do, dedicated to who we do it for, and proud of the trust placed in us.
